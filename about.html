About

Anthony Kean is a rising senior at the University of Miami in Coral Gables Florida. While at the University of Miami he is studying Sports Administration and Broadcast Journalism. He grew up in Colts Neck New Jersey and from a young age developed a passion for sports playing football, baseball and basketball. Currently he covers the University of Miami's top Division-1 Athletics for the Miami Hurricane student newspaper.
